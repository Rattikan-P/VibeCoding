Project Overview
This project builds a minimalist "Smart Parking" puzzle game. The game challenges players to manage a limited docking area by matching car colors with passenger needs. The goal is to provide a clean, satisfying experience that reduces mental clutter rather than adding to it.

Core Mechanics:

Grid Lot: Cars of various colors parked in a tight space.
Color Matching: Move cars to a waiting zone to match the current passenger.
Limited Space: A "Waiting Zone" with only 5 slots. If it fills, the game ends.
Success: Clear the entire lot to win.
Core Feeling & Fantasy
Feeling: Calm focus, spatial relief, and the "click" of perfect organization.

Fantasy: You are the "Zen Controller" of a futuristic transport hub, turning chaos into a smooth, colorful flow.

Target Players: People seeking a "mental reset," lovers of organization, and casual puzzle fans.
